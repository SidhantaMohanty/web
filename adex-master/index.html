CASE STUDIES

Check out some of case studies

  • Jon Kabat-Zinn and Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR)

    BACKGROUND: Jon Kabat-Zinn developed MBSR in the late 1970s at the University of Massachusetts Medical Center.
    SOLUTOIN: MBSR combines mindfulness meditation and yoga to reduce stress.
    OUTCOME: Numerous studies have shown that MBSR significantly reduces stress, anxiety, and depression, and improves overall well-being.

  • Aetna's Corporate Wellness Program

    BACKGROUND: Aetna, a health insurance company, introduced a wellness program for its employees focused on mindfulness and yoga.
    SOLUTOIN: The program included mindfulness meditation and yoga sessions
    OUTCOME: Participants reported a 28% reduction in stress levels, 20% improvement in sleep quality, and significant reductions in healthcare costs.

  • The Blue Zones Project

    BACKGROUND: The Blue Zones Project studies regions where people live exceptionally long and healthy lives.
    SOLUTOIN: Lifestyle practices such as social engagement, plant-based diets, regular physical activity, and stress-reduction techniques like meditation.
    OUTCOME:These practices contribute to lower stress levels, better health, and increased longevity.
